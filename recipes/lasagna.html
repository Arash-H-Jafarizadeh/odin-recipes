Lasagna Recipe

Description

For a very simple, short lasagna, use no-boil (oven-ready) noodles to skip the boiling step and a high-quality jarred marinara sauce to save time.

Ingredients

  • 1 box (9 oz) No-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar (24 oz) Marinara or pasta sauce
  • 1 container (15 oz) Ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 lb Ground beef or Italian sausage (optional, browned and drained)

Steps

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Layer 1: Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of the dish. Lay 3–4 noodles on top.
  3. Layer 2: Spread about 1/3 of the ricotta over the noodles, then add a layer of meat (if using), more sauce, and a handful of mozzarella.
  4. Repeat: Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, meat/sauce, mozzarella) two more times until ingredients are used up.
  5. Finish: End with a final layer of noodles topped with the remaining sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella.
  6. Bake: Cover tightly with foil. Bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Rest: Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing to allow the layers to set.

Quick Tip:

If you want it even faster, try a Skillet Lasagna where you break the noodles into pieces and cook everything in one pan on the stovetop in about 30 minutes.
